# FUKUSHIMA H7 Anti-Jamming Flight Controller

## Price: $750

## Product Overview

Next-generation flight controller with 2.4GHz SX1280 LoRa anti-jamming technology.

## Key Features

- Anti-Jamming: SX1280 2.4GHz LoRa with frequency hopping
- MCU: STM32H743ZIT6 @ 480MHz
- Dual IMU: ICM-42688-P + BMI270
- Range: Up to 10km
- Sensitivity: -137dBm

## Applications

- Military & Defense operations
- GPS-denied environments
- Urban RF-interference areas
- Industrial inspection
